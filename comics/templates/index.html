Comics Release Notifier

Comics are kind of annoying to keep up with. There's new ones every week, and while it's nice if you remember to check the release list every week, you can still easily miss titles that you care about.

This app simply gives you email notifications whenever a new issue of a series you like comes out.

Step One: Add some titles.

Step Two: Subscribe.

Step Three: There is no step three.

You'll get an email every day that comics you have subscribed to are released. This usually means one email every Wednesday.

If you'd like to unsubscribe, just click the link at the bottom of your email.
