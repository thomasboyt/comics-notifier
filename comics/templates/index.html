Comics are annoying to keep up with. There's new issues every week, and while it's nice if you remember to check the release list every week, you can still easily miss titles that you care about.

This app emails you whenever series you follow have new releases. Like your own personal JARVIS, except without the accent. Or any of the cool stuff. But at least there's emails!

Step One: Add some titles.

Step Two: Subscribe.

Step Three: There is no step three.

You'll get an email every day that comics you have subscribed to are released. This usually means one email every Wednesday. If you'd like to change your subscriptions or unsubscribe, just click the link at the bottom of your email.
